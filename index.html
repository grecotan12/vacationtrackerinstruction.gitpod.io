How to Use Vacation Tracker

Sign Up

When you open the app. The app asks you to login. However, you need to create an account first.

You only allowed to sign up one time. You have to reinstall the app to change username and password

Click SIGN UP. A sign up page will open

Enter User Name

Enter Password

Confirm Password

Enter Your Full Name

Click SIGN UP. You will be directing back to the login page

Log in

Enter username and password you just signed up. Click LOGIN

OPTIONAL: you can choose Remember Me so you don't have to enter your account everytime you start the app.

USER CAN ADD MANY VACATION AS POSSIBLE

  • You can click on the add button at the bottom right corner to add vacation in the "vacation page"
  • Enter vacation title
  • Enter vacation place (where you will be staying such as hotel)
  • NOTE: if you submit (Click "Save Vacation") with empty title, place, app won't do anything and set display error
  • Click on the start date, an calender will be open, you can click on the date then the text will pop up with the format yyyy-mm-dd of your chose date
  • Click on the start date, an calender will be open, you can click on the date then the text will pop up with the format yyyy-mm-dd of your chose date
  • NOTE: if you submit the start date that is after the end date (Click "Save Vacation"), app won't do anything and set display error.
  • NOTE: the application also validate the dates for correct format, app set display error in real time.
  • NOTE: if title is empty, place is empty or dates are empty when submitting (Click "Save Vacation"), app won't do anything and set display error.
  • After all the inputs are good, you click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Save Vacation"
  • Wait until the App direct you back to "vacation page"
  • Vacation page now will display the new vacation added.

USER CAN ADD MANY EXCURSIONS AS POSSIBLE

  • Click on a specific vacation that is added
  • In its "vacation detail" page
  • Click on the add button at the bottom right corner
  • Enter excursion title
  • NOTE:if you submit (Click "Save Excursion") with empty title app won't do anything and set display error
  • Click on the date, an calender will be open, you can click on the date then the text will pop up with the format yyyy-mm-dd of your chose date
  • NOTE: the application also validate the date for correct format, app set display error in real time.
  • NOTE: if you submit (Click "Save Excursion") the date is not between the vacation start date and end date inclusively, app won't do anything and set display error.
  • NOTE: if title is empty/date is empty and you submit (Click "Save Excursion") , app won't do anything and set display error.
  • After all inputs are good, you click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Save Excursion"
  • Wait until the App direct you back to "vacation detail page"
  • Vacation detail now will display the new excursion added.

USER CAN EDIT VACATION

  • Click on a specific vacation in "vacation page"
  • App will direct you to its "vacation details"
  • Change title, place, start date, end date
  • NOTE: the app still do all the validations (In real time and when click "Save Vacation" ) like adding.
  • After change the desired fields
  • Click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Save Vacation"
  • Wait until the App direct you back to the "vacation page"
  • IF you changed the title
  • The title will display on the "vacation page"
  • Otherwise, you can click on the vacation and see if the other fields have updated.

USER CAN EDIT EXCURSION

  • Click on a specific vacation in "vacation page"
  • App will direct you to its "vacation details"
  • Click on any excursion you want to edit
  • Change the title, date
  • NOTE: the app still do all the validations (In real time and when click "Save Excursion" ) like adding.
  • After change the desired fields
  • Click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Save Excursion"
  • Wait until the App direct you back to the "vacation detail" page.
  • You can verify and see if the excursion title/date is update in the "vacation detail" page

USER CAN DELETE VACATION WHEN THE VACATION DOES NOT HAVE ANY ASSOCIATE EXCURSION

  • CLick on a specific vacation in "vacation page"
  • App will direct you to its "vacation details"
  • Click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Delete Vacation"
  • NOTE: if the vacation has existing excursions and you submit (Click "Delete Vacation"), the app won't delete and give error display to note user to delete its associated excursions first.
  • Wait until the app direct you back to "vacation page"
  • The vacation page no longer have the deleted vacation.

USER CAN DELETE EXCURSION

  • Click on a specific vacation in "vacation page"
  • App will direct you to its "vacation details"
  • Click on a excursion you want to delete
  • App will direct you to its "excursion details"
  • Click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Delete Excursion"
  • Wait until the app direct you back to "vacation details" page
  • The vacation details page no longer have the deleted excursion.

USER CAN SHARE VACATION AND EXCURSION DETAILS

  • Click on a specific vacation in "vacation page"
  • App will direct you to its "vacation details"
  • Click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Share Vacation"
  • Choose any app of share you want to share with such as Gmail, Message, etc.
  • A text of vacation and its associate excursion information will display in the app.

USER CAN CREATE NOTIFICATION FOR VACATION START DATE AND END DATE

  • Click on a specific vacation in "vacation page"
  • App will direct you to its "vacation details"
  • Click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Notify Start Date"/ "Notify End Date"
  • If today is the start date/end date
  • A notification will pop up "TODAY IS THE START DATE: " + title/ "TODAY IS THE END DATE: " + title

USER CAN CREATE NOTIFICATION FOR EXCURSION DATE

  • Click on a specific vacation in "vacation page"
  • App will direct you to its "vacation details"
  • Click on a specific excursion
  • App will direct you to its "excursion details"
  • Click on the 3 dots menu top right corner
  • Choose "Notify Excursion"
  • If today is the date
  • A notification will pop up "TODAY IS THE DATE: " + title
